How It Works

SIGN-UP

The first stage is all about you — the customer. In the very first meeting itself, we encourage our customers to talk about themselves, the things that move them, the things that their kids find excitable, etc. Knowing all this helps our designers to create a first-cut design presentation that best resonates with our clients’ likings & sensibilities.

DESIGN

The design phase involves sitting with your designer through multiple rounds of design iteration (functional and aesthetic). Your designer will then design your home for tomorrow by ensuring it is future-proof and will stand the test of time.

IMPLEMENTATION

A Project manager and his team of Implementation experts will be assigned to you to implement the interiors of your home. Everything will be delivered to your doorstep, installed, assembled, and is built to last, to help transform your design dream into reality.

WARRANTY

Furdo gives you a 5 year warranty on ‘woodwork’ because we understand how important it is for your ‘dream home’ to stand the mild ravages of time. Our materials are therefore sourced from the very best, giving you peace of mind, and a home that you can proudly call your own.
